OUR MISSION

To promote young men and women progress through learning, loving and serving thus molding students to become ethical, principled and responsible leaders

OUR VISION

Education geared at promoting integrated personal development. Each Student is challenged to work towards realizing his/her potential through developing intellectual, physical, emotional, and spiritual attributes resulting in becoming a better person, transformed and prosperous

OUR CORE VALUES

To provide high standard education enriched with Christian values to develop more responsible citizens

To impress on students the culture of taking initiative

To help students realize and develop their talents in full

To inspire the students into the spirit of service for others.
